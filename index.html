The fate of all weaklings is defeat

To become famous in the capital is the dream of any village guy. The main character, Tatsumi, was no exception. Deciding that he wants to save his village, he goes to the capital with his friends, because that's where it's easiest to make money — or rather, he thought so.On the very first day in the capital, he is tricked around his finger, leaving him without money. It would seem that everything is lost now...

No money, no job, absolutely nothing. However, he meets a kind aristocrat Maria, who agrees to shelter him. On the night of the same day, people invade the house of Tatsumi's new acquaintance or rather revolutionaries, known as the "Night Raid". They claim that Aria is actually a maniac in a skirt, and not who she pretends to be. Who will Tatsumi believe? Revolutionaries or the girl who helped him?
